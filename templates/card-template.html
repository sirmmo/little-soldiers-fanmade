Legend. Def 🛡 — a wound lands only when hits > Def; each Shield/Scout token = −1 (min 0). Tough ♥ — breaks when Hearts ≥ Tough. Rank — single chevron ⌃ = New Recruit (front), double ⌃⌃ = Super Soldiers (back). Actions — every Little Soldier has Attack + Move + one Special; the green tag is the unit's signature. ⚡ Ability on the back activates once per Lightning rolled (linear); the Move heal is universal. The 3+⚡ tier exists only on Threats. Tactics (purple) and Objects (teal) come from the Modules book.
